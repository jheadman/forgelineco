About Forgeline

Intelligence that moves at the speed of deals

Forgeline was built on a simple observation: the companies and investors who win aren't the ones with the most data — they're the ones who see what others miss. We combine deep industrial sector knowledge with analytical rigor to deliver intelligence that changes how clients see their markets.

We work at the intersection of competitive intelligence and strategic advisory. Engagements start with research — the kind that surfaces non-obvious insights about competitors, supply chains, and market structure. When that research reveals strategic implications, we help clients act on them.

Our clients are PE deal teams evaluating acquisitions, mid-market industrial companies navigating competitive shifts, and family offices seeking an analytical edge. We're built for speed, depth, and discretion.
